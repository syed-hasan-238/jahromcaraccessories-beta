The Origin

BORN IN
IRAN.
BUILT IN
SHARJAH.

The story of Juhrum begins not in the UAE, but in Iran — a country with one of the most passionate car cultures in the world. Founded by Hadi Ghanaatian, Juhrum carries the DNA of Persian attention to detail: the belief that anything worth doing is worth doing perfectly.

When Hadi established the shop in Sharjah's Industrial Area 4, he brought that same obsession with him. He knew exactly what serious truck owners wanted — not just parts, but accessories that genuinely elevated their vehicle. Products chosen with care, fitted with precision, backed with knowledge.

More than five years later, Juhrum has become the go-to destination for pickup truck owners across the UAE who refuse to compromise.

02
The Environment

BUILT FOR
UAE
TERRAIN.

The UAE is one of the most demanding environments on earth for vehicles and their accessories. Summer temperatures exceed 48°C. Sand finds its way into every seal, every joint, every mechanism. Off-road terrain can destroy inferior products within a season.

Every product at Juhrum is selected with this environment in mind. Bed covers that seal against desert dust. Bull bars rated for rocky wadi approaches. PPF that withstands UAE heat and UV without yellowing or peeling. Thermal insulation that genuinely reduces cabin temperature.

If it can't survive a full UAE summer, it doesn't earn a place in our showroom.

03
The Products

WHAT WE
ACTUALLY
DO.

Juhrum specialises in American pickup trucks — Ford F-Series, Dodge Ram, GMC Sierra, and the broader family of 4×4 off-roaders that UAE drivers love. Our range covers everything a serious truck owner could need:

Bed covers — from retractable hardtops in 6063-T5 aluminium to soft tri-fold covers with weather-tight seals. Exterior builds — bull bars, running boards, roof racks, fender flares. Electronics — head units with Apple CarPlay, premium audio, cameras. Protection — self-healing PPF, thermal insulation, concealed protection. Interior upholstery — full leather seat and floor installations in any colour.

And because we source directly from manufacturers, our prices remain competitive without compromising on quality. That's the promise Hadi made when he opened the doors — and it's the promise Juhrum keeps every day.
