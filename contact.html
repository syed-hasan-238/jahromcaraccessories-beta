HOW TO

01

Take Emirates Road (E611) or Al Dhaid Road (E88) toward Sharjah Industrial Areas. Exit toward Industrial Area 4 — we're on First Industrial Street, building 274.

02

We're located in Industrial Area 4, immediately after the ADNOC petrol pump on First Industrial Street. Look for the shop on your left — you'll see trucks being fitted outside.

Free parking available directly outside the showroom. We have space to manoeuvre trucks and large vehicles. Open 8AM to 11PM every day — no appointment needed, just come in.
